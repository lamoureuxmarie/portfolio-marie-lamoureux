Logo "Les Mousseux"

"Les Mousseux" is a group of senior surfers, from La Martinique, who like to hang out and drink beer, after a good session of surf. They asked me to design this logo for them. I really like the wordplay they're using for their name, a "mousse" being either a beer or a wave, in French language. I tried to convey this in my design.
