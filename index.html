    My Kita App

    My kita is a application designed to simplify the search of a kindergarten in Berlin, by helping parents be more organised and keeping track of their progress.

    This app was imagined by myself in the course as part of my final project for Le Wagon Bootcamp.

    Two of my classmates, Missato and Daniel, built it with me.

    The app includes a search tool and map - to locate the kitas in Berlin - a task manager. It allows to save email templates and send them. As well as the possibility to set reminders, that the user receives on his Telegram. The kitas informations are scraped from the Kita Navigator Berlin.
