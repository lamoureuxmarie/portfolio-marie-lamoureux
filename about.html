About me

Front-end developer & designer from Guéthary, France.

Hey,

My name is Marie. I'm 29-year-old, from the South West of France and living in Berlin since 2017.

I've studied graphic design in Dublin, and worked on several freelance design projects, which I really enjoyed. But, graphic design alone wasn't enough for me to thrive in my professional life. That's why I decided to learn coding, and I've recently completed the SheCodes course.

I'd described myself as a creative person, who always likes to explore new styles and learn new things. My goal is to guide the client with my expertise, to create a smart and clean design, yet always try to meet their expectations.

I'm now looking forward to use my knowledge in graphic design, as well as my programming skills in future projects!
